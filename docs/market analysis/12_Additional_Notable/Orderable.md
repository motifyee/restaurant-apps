## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Orderable provides a WordPress-based online ordering system for restaurants already using or wanting to build on WordPress/WooCommerce.

**Market Fit:** High for WordPress restaurants. Very Low for non-WP operations.

**Best For**: WordPress restaurants and WooCommerce stores wanting ordering systems.

**Not For**: Non-WordPress restaurants or operations not wanting to manage WordPress.

## Technical Requirements

### To Use TastyIgniter
- **Web Server**: Apache, Nginx, or similar
- **PHP**: Version 7.4 or higher
- **MySQL**: Version 5.7 or higher
- **Composer**: Dependency management
- **Domain Name**: For website
- **SSL Certificate**: For secure payments

### Optional But Recommended
- **CodeIgniter**: Framework knowledge
- **PHP Skills**: For customization
- **Server Management**: Basic Linux skills

## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** TastyIgniter is the only free, open-source, self-hosted restaurant online ordering system. Perfect for tech-savvy operators wanting full control and zero licensing costs.

**Market Fit**: High for developers and tech-savvy restaurants. Very Low for non-technical owners.

**Key Differentiator**: Only free, open-source, self-hosted restaurant ordering system with MIT license for commercial use.

**Best For**: Developers, tech-savvy restaurant owners, agencies, and operations with IT resources wanting full control and zero licensing costs.

**Not For**: Non-technical restaurant owners, operations without IT resources, or those wanting turnkey cloud solutions.

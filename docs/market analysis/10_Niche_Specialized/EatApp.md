## Core Features

### Reservation System
- **Online Reservations**: Book tables
- **Table Management**: Optimize seating
- **Waitlist**: Handle crowds
- **Guest Database**: Customer profiles
- **No-Show Prevention**: Reduce cancellations

### Pay at Table
- **QR Code Widgets**: Scan to view/pay
- **Mobile Payments**: Pay from phone
- **Bill Splitting**: Flexible payment
- **Tip Management**: Gratuity handling
- **Receipts**: Digital receipts

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Reservations First**: Core strength
2. **Pay at Table**: Integrated payments
3. **Asian Focus**: Regional expertise
4. **Complete System**: Reservations + payments
5. **Guest Data**: Customer insights

### Competitive Disadvantages
1. **Reservation Focused**: QR is add-on
2. **Regional Focus**: Asia-Pacific
3. **Undisclosed Pricing**: No public rates
4. **Platform Complexity**: Learning curve

## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** EatApp combines restaurant reservations with pay at table QR functionality, focused on Asian-Pacific markets with complete guest management.

**Market Fit**: Medium for reservation-focused restaurants.

**Best For**: Restaurants with reservations wanting integrated pay-at-table QR widgets.

**Not For**: Quick-service or walk-in only establishments.

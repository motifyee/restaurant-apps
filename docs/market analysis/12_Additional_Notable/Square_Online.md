## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Square Online offers truly free online ordering with QR menus ($0 monthly fees) but requires Square payment processing (2.6% + 10¢). Ideal for new/small restaurants wanting no monthly costs.

**Market Fit**: Very High for small restaurants and startups. Low for high-volume operations (processing fees add up).

**Key Differentiator**: $0 monthly fees with integrated Square payments and fast deposits.

**Best For**: New restaurant startups, small restaurants (1-5 locations), cafes, food trucks, and operations already using Square POS.

**Not For**: High-volume restaurants (processing fees significant), operations wanting payment processor choice, or restaurants outside Square's geographic coverage.

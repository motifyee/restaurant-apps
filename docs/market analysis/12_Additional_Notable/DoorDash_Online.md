## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** DoorDash Online Ordering provides commission-based (15-30%) direct ordering with access to DoorDash's 20+ million customers and built-in delivery network. No monthly fees but high commissions.

**Market Fit**: Medium for restaurants wanting delivery access. Low for margin-conscious operations.

**Key Differentiator**: Access to DoorDash's massive customer base (20M+) and delivery network with pay-per-order model.

**Best For**: Restaurants wanting delivery access, operations without delivery infrastructure, and startups wanting quick launch on DoorDash platform.

**Not For**: Restaurants protecting margins, operations wanting to control customer data, or high-volume venues (commissions become prohibitive).

**Warning**: 15-30% commission fees significantly erode restaurant margins. Calculate carefully before committing.

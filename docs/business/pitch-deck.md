**THE PROBLEM**
### Egyptian restaurants are losing 15-25% of revenue to delivery platform commissions

**Delivery Platforms (Talabat, Otta)**
- 15-25% commission per order
- Designed for delivery, not dine-in
- Not sustainable for thin restaurant margins

**Traditional POS Systems**
- Expensive hardware and setup
- No customer-facing features
- Too complex for small restaurants

**Result**
Restaurants have NO affordable digital ordering solution for dine-in customers

There's simply no affordable, commission-free solution designed specifically for Egyptian dine-in restaurants."

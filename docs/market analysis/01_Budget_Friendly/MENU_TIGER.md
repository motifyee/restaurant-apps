## Market Position & Competition

### Primary Competitors
- **UpMenu**: Higher pricing, commission-free ordering
- **FineDine**: Premium features, AI recommendations
- **QReveal**: Similar freemium model at $19/mo
- **Oddmenu**: Free trial, real-time editing
- **Menu Tz**: From $6.99/mo for bars/cafes

### Competitive Advantages
1. **True Freemium**: Generous free tier with 10 QR codes
2. **Multilingual**: Auto language detection (unique feature)
3. **No Commissions**: Keep 100% of order revenue
4. **Flexible Pricing**: $0-119/month range
5. **14-Day Trials**: Risk-free testing
6. **No Contracts**: Month-to-month flexibility

### Competitive Disadvantages
1. **Lower Brand Recognition**: Less known than UpMenu/FineDine
2. **Limited Integrations**: Fewer POS connections
3. **Basic Analytics**: Advanced insights in premium only
4. **No Mobile App**: Web-only solution

## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** MENU TIGER offers one of the most generous freemium models in the QR menu space, with unique multilingual capabilities that make it ideal for hotels and tourist-area restaurants.

**Market Fit:** High for small restaurants and hospitality venues. Medium for larger operations.

**Key Differentiator:** Automatic multilingual menu support and generous free tier.

**Best For:** Small restaurants, hotels, cafes, and venues with international customers.

**Not For:** Large enterprise chains or operations requiring extensive native integrations and mobile apps.

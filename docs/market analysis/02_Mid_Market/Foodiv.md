## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Foodiv offers a genuinely free plan with unlimited orders, menu items, and QR scans - a rare find in the market. Its commission-free model with free setup makes it attractive for budget-conscious restaurants.

**Market Fit:** High for small restaurants wanting unlimited free features. Medium for growing operations.

**Key Differentiator**: Truly free plan with unlimited orders, items, and scans.

**Best For:** Small restaurants, budget-conscious operations, and high-volume restaurants wanting free solution.

**Not For:** Enterprise chains or operations requiring advanced features.

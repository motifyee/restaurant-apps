## Market Insights Summary

### Pricing Trends
1. **Freemium Growth**: More platforms offering free tiers (GloriaFood, MENU TIGER, Foodiv)
2. **Commission-Free**: Major differentiator (UpMenu, CloudWaitress, Restolabs, Foodiv)
3. **Transparent Pricing**: Customer demand (CloudWaitress leads, Sociavore $49/mo flat)
4. **Annual vs Monthly**: Mix of flexibility (Sociavore monthly) vs. contracts (BentoBox, Toast)

### Feature Differentiation Leaders

| Feature | Market Leaders |
|---------|----------------|
| **Mobile Apps Included** | UpMenu, FineDine |
| **Marketing Automation** | Popmenu, SpotHopper |
| **Multi-Language** | TableQR, MENU TIGER |
| **AI Analytics** | FineDine, eMenu AI |
| **POS Integration** | Deliverect (175+), Olo, Oracle Simphony, Toast |
| **Hotel Specialization** | QReveal Hotels, TapDodo |
| **Delivery Aggregation** | Deliverect (175+ platforms) |
| **White-Label** | Restolabs, Horago, FoodChow |
| **Kiosk Hardware** | Grubbrr, Toast, Eats365, Jamezz |

### Market Gaps / Opportunities

1. **Food Trucks & Pop-Ups**: Few specialized solutions
2. **Catering Companies**: Limited dedicated platforms (Ordering.co notable exception)
3. **Regional Platforms**: Many opportunities outside US/EU
4. **Offline-First**: Few work reliably without internet
5. **Sustainability Branding**: Eco-features rare
6. **Accessibility**: Voice ordering, screen readers uncommon
7. **Micro-Restaurants**: Under $30/mo options limited
8. **Menu Design Assistance**: Many platforms lack professional design help

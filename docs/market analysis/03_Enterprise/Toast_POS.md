**Pricing Model**: Custom quote based on business size

**Market Estimates:**
- **Hardware Kit**: $2,000-10,000+ one-time

- **Payment Processing**: 2.4-3.0% + $0.15 per transaction

### Pricing Tiers (Typical Structure)
- **Starter**: Free software with payment processing
- **Essentials**: ~$65-110/month per terminal
- **Growth**: ~$110-165/month per terminal
- **Custom**: Enterprise pricing

5. **Complex**: More features than needed for small restaurants

## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Toast is the leading all-in-one restaurant platform in the US, serving 70,000+ locations with comprehensive POS, online ordering, delivery, payments, and management tools. Its restaurant-first design, hardware quality, and complete ecosystem justify premium pricing for serious restaurant operations.

**Market Fit:** Very High for full-service and fast-casual restaurants. Medium for very small operations. Low for takeout-only or temporary operations.

**Key Differentiator**: Complete restaurant ecosystem (POS + hardware + online ordering + delivery + payments) from a public market leader.

**Best For:** Full-service restaurants, fast-casual concepts, multi-location groups (5+ locations), and operations wanting an all-in-one solution with strong support.

**Not For:** Very small operations (under $50k/month revenue), businesses with tight budgets, or restaurants wanting payment processor flexibility.

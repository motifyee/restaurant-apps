## Core Features

### Consumer App
- **Menu Search**: Search by dish, not just restaurant
- **Menu Discovery**: Find specific foods
- **Restaurant Menus**: Browse menus
- **Location-Based**: Find nearby options
- **User Reviews**: Menu item ratings

### Restaurant Features
- **Menu Display**: Digital menu hosting
- **Analytics**: What customers search
- **Insights**: Popular dishes
- **Customer Data**: Dining preferences

---

## Business Model

### Two-Sided Platform
- **Consumer Side**: Free app with menu search
- **Restaurant Side**: Analytics and insights
- **Revenue Model**: Ads, partnerships, premium features

---

## Market Position & Competition

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Menu Search**: Unique search-by-dish
2. **Consumer App**: B2C focus
3. **Menu Insights**: Restaurant analytics
4. **Discovery**: New customer acquisition
5. **Data-Driven**: Menu intelligence

### Competitive Disadvantages
1. **Consumer Focus**: Not traditional B2B
2. **Discovery Platform**: Not full ordering
3. **Dependent on Users**: Requires adoption
4. **Limited Control**: Restaurants less control

---

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Best Fit For
- Restaurants wanting discovery
- Establishments with unique dishes
- Operations wanting menu insights
- Customer acquisition focus

### Not Ideal For
- Private ordering systems
- Operations not wanting discovery
- Menu-only needs

---

## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Sirved is a menu-based search engine and consumer app allowing users to search by dish rather than restaurant, offering restaurants discovery and menu insights.

**Market Fit**: Medium for restaurants wanting customer discovery.

**Best For**: Restaurants wanting discovery and menu insights.

**Not For**: Operations wanting private ordering systems or not seeking discovery.

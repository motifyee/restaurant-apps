## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Include numbers when possible
- Personalize with recipient name or company
- Avoid spammy words (free, guarantee, urgent)
- Test different variations

### Body Best Practices
- Keep it under 200 words
- One clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly formatting
- Personalize opening line
- Show social proof when available

### Timing Recommendations
| Segment | Best Day | Best Time |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Investors | Tuesday-Thursday | 9-11 AM |
| Restaurant Owners | Monday-Wednesday | 2-4 PM |
| Partnerships | Tuesday-Thursday | 10 AM-12 PM |

### Follow-Up Sequence
```
Day 0: Initial email
Day 3: First follow-up (brief)
Day 7: Second follow-up (add value)
Day 14: Final follow-up (breakup email)
```

### Customer Lifetime Value
```
Average Revenue per Customer: 67.5 EGP/month
Annual Revenue per Customer: 810 EGP
Customer Lifetime (at 10% annual churn): 10 years
LTV = 810 EGP × 10 = 8,100 EGP
```

## Breakeven Analysis

### Fixed Costs (Monthly)

| Cost Item | Monthly (EGP) | Annual (EGP) |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Server/Infrastructure | 3,000 | 36,000 |
| Software licenses | 1,500 | 18,000 |
| Accounting/Legal | 2,000 | 24,000 |
| Office (if needed) | 0 | 0 (remote) |
| **Total Fixed** | **6,500** | **78,000** |

### Variable Costs (Per Customer)

| Cost Item | Per Customer (EGP) | % of Revenue |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| Payment processing | 2 | 3% |
| Support | 5 | 7% |
| **Total Variable** | **7** | **10%** |

### Breakeven Calculation

```
Contribution Margin = 67.5 EGP - 7 EGP = 60.5 EGP/customer
Breakeven Customers = 6,500 EGP ÷ 60.5 EGP = 108 customers
Breakeven Revenue = 108 × 67.5 × 12 = 87,480 EGP/year
```

**Breakeven Achieved: Month 11 of Year 1 ✅**

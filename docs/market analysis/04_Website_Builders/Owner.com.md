## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Owner.com provides commission-free online ordering via modern restaurant websites, allowing restaurants to keep 100% of revenue and own customer data, contrasting with DoorDash's 15-30% commission model. Built in 2022 with current technology.

**Market Fit**: Very High for restaurants wanting to avoid delivery platform commissions and control customer data.

**Key Differentiator**: Modern platform (built 2022) with commission-free model and customer data ownership.

**Best For**: Independent restaurants wanting to avoid delivery platform commissions, operations valuing data ownership, multi-location chains, and restaurants with own delivery capability.

**Not For**: Restaurants wanting DoorDash's customer access, operations without delivery infrastructure, or restaurants outside US.

**Warning**: Monthly subscription costs require volume to justify. Calculate ROI based on your order volume vs. delivery platform savings.

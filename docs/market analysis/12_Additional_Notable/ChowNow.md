## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** ChowNow offers commission-free online ordering as an independent platform, allowing restaurants to keep 100% of revenue and own customer data, contrasting with DoorDash's 15-30% commission model.

**Market Fit**: Very High for restaurants wanting to avoid commissions and control customer data.

**Key Differentiator**: Commission-free model with customer data ownership, independent of delivery platform control.

**Best For**: Independent restaurants wanting to avoid delivery platform commissions and own customer relationships.

**Not For**: Restaurants wanting DoorDash's customer access or those without delivery infrastructure.

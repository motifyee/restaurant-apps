## Competitive Verdict

**Overall Assessment:** Olo is the undisputed leader in enterprise restaurant digital ordering, powering 80,000+ locations for 100+ major brands including Five Guys, Shake Shack, and Chipotle. Its public company status, Olo Pay platform, and data ownership model justify premium pricing for massive chains.

**Market Fit:** Very High for enterprise chains (100+ locations). Not suitable for independent or small chain restaurants.

**Key Differentiator**: Enterprise-grade platform with 80,000+ locations, Olo Pay, and major brand portfolio including Five Guys and Shake Shack.

**Best For:** Major chains (100+ locations), large regional groups (50+ locations), high-volume operations, and enterprise restaurant groups.

**Not For:** Independent restaurants, small chains (under 10 locations), or mid-sized groups (10-50 locations) - Olo is enterprise-only.
